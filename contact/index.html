No crazy forms to fill out or captchas to make you go cross-eyed.
We know your time is valuable.

Fire up your favorite email app and let us know what you're seeking. The Training address at github.com will reach out to the entire Training team. We'll get back to you as soon as we've got feet on the ground. (We fly a lot.)

Some things you might want to let us know:

  • How many people that you'd like to have attend the training.
  • General level of current Git usage. (Use it multiple times a day, Occasional commits to a Git repo, "Git What?!")
  • What you currently use for Version Control.
  • Any notes on your workflow that you think we should know. (It helps us tailor the curriculum for your course.)
  • Anything else you'd like to share!

We will work with you to determine the best options for training for your team.

If you have a question about GitHub Training that isn't related to organizing a private training event, no worries. Feel free to reach out to us too! If you've got general questions about GitHub, drop a line to GitHub Support.
